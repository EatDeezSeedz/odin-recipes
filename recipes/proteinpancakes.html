Protein Pancakes

Description

This recipe is makes for a delicious breakfast for anyone trying to get protein in. These pancakes contain 70 grams of protein. You may add whatever toppings you'd like to it. Perfect for a bulk

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 60 grams protein powder
  • 1 cup oat flour (80 grams)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Toppings of your choice
