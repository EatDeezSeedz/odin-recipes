Chocolate Protein Oatmeal Recipe

Description

This tasty breakfast is easy to make and perfect for a bulk. Only contains four ingredients. Has 990 calories and 75 grams of protein.

Ingredients

  • Two servings of oats
  • One cup chocolate protein milk
  • One serving of chocolate protein powder
  • Two servings of peanut butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix oats with the chocolate milk.
  2. Microwave for two minutes
  3. Add the protein powder and peanut butter and mix
  4. That's it! Enjoy!
