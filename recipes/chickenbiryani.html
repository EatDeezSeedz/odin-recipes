Chicken Biryani Recipe

Description

Chicken biryani is a mixed rice dish that originates from Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made of indian spices, rice, and chicken.

Ingredients

  • Two lbs chicken
  • One cup plain yogurt
  • One tablespoon paprika
  • One teaspoon tumeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Two red onions
  • Three tomatoes (sliced)
  • One tablespoon ginger paste
  • One tablespoon garlic paste
  • Two teaspoons garam masala
  • One cup water (for chicken)
  • More water (for rice)
  • Bay leaves
  • Cloves
  • Star Anise
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Cardamoms
  • Two cups basmati rice

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add chicken, plain yogurt, paprika, tumeric, and salt to taste.
  2. Mix it and marinate for one hour.
  3. Once marinated, oil your pan and caramelize your onions.
  4. Once caramelized, remove onions and add the chicken to the same pan.
  5. Once the chicken has a nice crust on one side, flip it and add your tomatoes, ginger paste, garlic paste, garam masala and mix.
  6. Bring back onions, add one cup water and cook till tender.
  7. In a seperate pot, add water, bay leaves, cloves, star anise, cinnamon stick, cardamom, and your rice.
  8. Once the rice is cooked, add it to the chicken, and add a pinch of salt.
  9. Enjoy!
